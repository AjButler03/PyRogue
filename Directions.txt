 Welcome, traveler.
 
 You awake in a dimly lit dungeon, not remembering who you are, and not
 knowing what to do. In the darkness, you hear shuffling movement and 
 disgusting, gutteral, animal-like sounds. In the background there might also
 be. . . is that right?. . . the childish laughter of Tom Kenny, but with 
 a chilling malice behind it. 
 
 You know that you are not safe; it is your job to survive.

 There are items scattered around the dungeon to help you; these will be 
 pivotal to your survival. They will increase your speed, your ability to 
 dodge damage, and you defense against attacks.
 Without them, you are as good as dead.
 
 You have 10 carry slots to pickup and hold these items, and you have equip 
 slots for weapon, offhand, ranged, armor, helmet, cloak, gloves, boots, 
 amulet, light, and left & right rings.

 Know that the dungeon is unforgiving; you never know what lurks in the 
 darkness, and being caught unprepared is a sure way to die.

 Good luck.

================================   MOVEMENT   ================================
  7 or y : Attempt to move PC one cell to the upper left.
  8 or k : Attempt to move PC one cell up.
  9 or u : Attempt to move PC one cell to the upper right.
  6 or l : Attempt to move PC one cell to the right.
  3 or n : Attempt to move PC one cell to the lower right.
  2 or j : Attempt to move PC one cell down.
  1 or b : Attempt to move PC one cell to the lower left.
  4 or h : Attempt to move PC one cell to the left.
  > : Attempt to go down stairs. Works only if standing on down staircase.
  < : Attempt to go up stairs. Works only if standing on up staircase.
  5 or space or . : Rest for a turn NPCs still move.

================================   INVENTORY  ================================
  m : Display a list of monsters in the dungeon, with their symbol and 
      position relative to the PC (e.g., "c, 2 north and 14 west").
  i : Gives item names that are in each of the PC's carry slots.
  e : Opens a menu that lists the item names that are equipped by the PC.
  , : Attempts to pick up an item that the PC is standing over, placing in 
      next available carry slot. Will display message on success/failure.
  w : Asks for a carry slot, then attempts to equip the item in that slot.
  t : Aks for an equipment slot, then attempts move it to carry slot. 
  x : Asks for a carry slot, then attempts to destroy  the specified item.
  d : Asks for a carry slot, then attempts to drop the specified item.

================================  INFORMATION ================================
  L : Enters target mode; 'L' again shows information about targeted monster.
  I : Asks for carry slot, then displays information about it.

================================    CHEATS    ================================
  f : Toggles the 'fog of war' effect.
  g : Enters teleport mode to allow PC to move across the map; Pressing 'g' 
      again teleports to cursor location; 'r' teleports to a random location.
  z : Shows distance maps, or shows monster's path while inspecting one.

================================ MISCELLANOUS ================================
  up arrow : Scrolls menu up, if scrolling is available/ necessary.
  down arrow: Scrolls menu down, if scrolling is available/ necessary.
  esc : Returns to character control from the various menus.
  Q : Quit to main menu or quit game.
